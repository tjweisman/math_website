About me

I am a fourth-year mathematics PhD candidate at the University of Texas at Austin. Currently my research interests are in convex projective structures and geometric group theory. My PhD advisor is Jeff Danciger. I graduated with a bachelor's in mathematics from Yale University in 2016.
