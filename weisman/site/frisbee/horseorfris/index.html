"Horse or frisbee team" created by Teddy Weisman.

Horse names are all historical Kentucky Derby contenders, scraped from Wikipedia. Frisbee team names are from the 2017 USAU club season, scraped from the list here in summer 2017. I personally selected what I thought were the 200 or so least obvious names in each category.
